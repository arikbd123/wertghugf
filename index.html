STATEMENT:

IntraConnection Enterprises aims to be a social club focused around creating meaningful products. If a physical product or 2 becomes real along the way so be it. Airdrops and continuous art are fun but not sustainable. ICE intends to create a pathway forward enabling a small team of creators to do what they do best creating without requiring our community to continuously front funds to continue the next step.




VALUES:

Authenticity, Interaction, Grit, Inspiration




TOKENOMICS:

$ICE will represent a piece of the IntraConnection Enterprises such that all tokens will be direct ownership providing additional incentive for our members to provide public support in our marketing efforts. ICE to be deployed on the Cardano network with 20% allocation to Building Block minters. Token supply will be 43,560,000 providing 14,520 to each Building Blocks series mint (1/3,000 total supply). ICE will be the all encompassing umbrella token for the ecosystem. 80% will be reserved for future distributions at future market price allowing further raises if necessary for extremely ambitious projects. Secondly reserved tokens would be used to add liquidity to the token if at some point price becomes a barrier. 10% will be distributed monthly over 2 yrs to top 10 social account growers (only invites through preset individual invite links will be tracked). ICE at this time doesn't provide explicit voting rights as the team becomes more sophisticated this may be adjusted for enterprise longevity.
